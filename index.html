About Me

I am Raj Patel Rajkot, Gujarat the colourful state of India. Gujarat is famous for its food through worldwide but present Gujarat is also famous for its digital marketing skills and upcoming talents. Some years ago if I had said that I wanted to opt digital marketing as a career, I am sure no one would have supported me in the decision. But today is the wave of digitalisation. Many young talents are now taking s as a full time profession. I am happy to see this change, it is bringing India towards development.

I am currently working at Soarbeam digital. It is a digital marketing company providing place to various brands, influencers and learners at one place. People working here are highly educated in their field so it is work plus leaning opportunity for me.

One thing about digital marketing is that it requires quality content and consistency. I have been regularly trying to give my best on various social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. My SEO Training was started in March 2020, before that I was self learning on the subject. This helped in taking my content creation work to next levels. Once we get the proper knowledge their application becomes very easy. Therefore I always ask everyone to get into the ocean of knowledge and keep learning.

My current working area is PPC ( Pay Per Click ) Under the following platforms -

Google Ads
